Place your drone.glb model here as: /public/drone.glb

The model should have the following named objects for propeller animation:
- Propeller_1
- Propeller_2
- Propeller_3
- Propeller_4
- Propeller_5
- Propeller_6

Recommended sources (free agricultural/hexacopter drone models):
- Sketchfab: https://sketchfab.com/search?q=agricultural+drone&type=models&sort_by=-likeCount&features=downloadable
- Free3D: https://free3d.com/
- TurboSquid (free section)

For best performance keep the model under 5MB.
Run: npx gltfjsx public/drone.glb to inspect node names after placing the file.
